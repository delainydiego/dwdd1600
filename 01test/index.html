Digital Citizenship

What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship is the idea of using technology—especially the internet—in a responsible, safe, ethical, and effective way.

What is digital law?

Digital law refers to the legal rights and restrictions governing technology use.

What is AUP

An Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is a set of rules that outlines how technology resources should be used appropriately and responsibly.

What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is the act of using someone else's work or ideas without giving them credit.

Plagiarism can include copying and pasting text from a source without citation, paraphrasing someone else's work without acknowledgment, or presenting someone else's ideas as your own.

What are the rules for AI generated content?

AI generated content should be used ethically and responsibly, with proper attribution and transparency about the use of AI tools.
